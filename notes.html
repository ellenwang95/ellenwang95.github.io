Note To Self

Things I try to keep in mind

Do more, think less

Make opportunities

Don't wait for them to come to you. Pay attention and know your potential.

Take initiative

Reach out first. Be helpful. Speak up. Make it your responsibility.

Improve yourself

Don't point fingers. What happened? Why did it happen? What can you do differently next time?

Know your limits

You know yourself best. Make your own judgment calls.
